IT Aides

We believe that Information Technology (IT) Aide classification is a “launching point” for LA County careers in the technology field. This page provides an analysis of this position, as well as positions that “feed” into the IT Aide, often referred to as “feeder classes”. The analysis takes a look at incumbents and vacancies as of August 1, 2018 and hiring trends from the previous three (3) years (Aug 1 2015-Jul 31 2018).

What is the number of vacant IT Aide positions?

There are 13 vacant IT Aide positions across the county, all of which are full-time permanent positions. In addition, there are currently 54 currently filled positions. The overall vacancy rate is 19.4%.

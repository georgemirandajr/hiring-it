IT Positions in LA County

Types of positions

There are 3,030 IT positions in the County based on the Information Technology Series within Schedule A provided by CEO. A high-level summary of these positions is included below.

  • Nearly all 34 departments have at least one of these IT positions in their workforce, the exceptions being the Museum of Art and Museum of Natrual History.
  • These positions fall under a range of categories beyond the purely technical. Based on the EEO job categories, there are hundreds in administrative or management roles.
  • The vast majority of IT positions are full-time permanent (2,993 full-time permanent positions equate to 99%)
